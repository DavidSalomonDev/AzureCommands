---
title: Listar almacenes de Recovery Services
description: Muestra los Recovery Services Vault de un grupo de recursos.
shell: powershell
category: Recovery Services Vault
tags: [backup, recovery, vault, listar, consulta]
template: "Get-AzRecoveryServicesVault -ResourceGroupName <resourceGroup> | Format-Table Name, Location, Type"
parameters:
  - name: resourceGroup
    label: Grupo de recursos
    type: string
    required: true
    placeholder: mi-grupo-recursos
---

Omite `-ResourceGroupName` para listar todos los almacenes de la suscripción.
